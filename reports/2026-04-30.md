## AI & Technology

### Google TPU 8: Two Chips for the Agentic Era
- **Google announced 8th-gen TPUs** at Cloud Next — first time splitting into two purpose-built chips:
  - **TPU 8t (Sunfish)**: training chip, designed with Broadcom. 9,600-chip superpods, 121 ExaFlops, 2PB shared HBM. 2.7x better training cost-efficiency vs TPU v7e. ([Google Blog](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/infrastructure-and-cloud/google-cloud/eighth-generation-tpu-agentic-era/))
  - **TPU 8i (Zebrafish)**: inference chip, designed with MediaTek. 288GB HBM + 384MB SRAM, 3x compute per pod vs prior gen. 5x lower on-chip latency. ([The Next Web](https://thenextweb.com/news/google-ironwood-tpu-inference-cloud-next))
  - Both on **TSMC 2nm** process, targeting late 2027 GA.
- **Anthropic deal expanded to 3.5GW of compute** coming online in 2027 — anchor customer for both TPU generations.
- **MediaTek** requested 7x increase in TSMC CoWoS advanced packaging capacity for TPU 8i.
- **Implication**: TSMC's 2nm order book filling up. Broadcom and MediaTek both gaining AI custom chip design revenue.
