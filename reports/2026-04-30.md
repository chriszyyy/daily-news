## China News & Economy

### Q1 GDP & Macro
- **China Q1 GDP: 5.0% YoY**, beating expectations of 4.8%, up from 4.5% in Q4 2025. Strongest in 3 quarters. Driven by exports (+14.7% YoY in Q1). ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/chinas-economy-poised-q1-rebound-iran-war-jolts-2026-outlook-2026-04-15/), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/16/china-gdp-growth-first-quarter-exports-property-retail-sales-iran-war.html))

- **Retail sales slowed to 1.7% YoY in March** (from 2.8% in Jan-Feb) — consumption remains the weak link.
- **Factory-gate prices turned positive** in March for first time in 3+ years — energy costs seeping into manufacturing.
